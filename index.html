About StudentScan Pro

StudentScan Pro is a high-utility academic tool designed for students worldwide. We help bridge the gap between handwritten notes and professional academic submissions. Our mission is to provide a fast, secure, and easy-to-use platform that automates the formatting of multi-page PDF documents with professional headers, ensuring students can focus on their learning rather than formatting.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data. Your privacy is our absolute priority.
